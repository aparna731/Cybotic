Sentia Web Design

2/4/23

On the occasion of Sentia 2023, the Web programming competition was hosted by CS(IOT) on the second day of Sentia. The theme was “Our Great India,” and the participants were challenged to bring out the uniqueness in their web page by selecting something related to Indian culture as a model and also bringing practicality to their work. After summarizing the theme and rules, the participants were allowed one and a half hours to complete their design. The event was conducted under supervision, and after the time limit, the designs were judged based on criteria of Aesthetics & creativity, implementation of the theme, functionality, content, and impact.
The competition buzzed with excitement as participants delved into creating web pages that would encapsulate the essence of India's rich culture and heritage. Armed with the theme "Our Great India," they set out to showcase India's diversity and practicality in their web designs. Within the allocated time frame of one and a half hours, participants worked diligently to breathe life into their creative visions, and the atmosphere was charged with innovation and ingenuity.
Overseeing the competition ensured that the event flowed seamlessly, and as the clock ticked, the air was filled with anticipation. The competition came to an exciting conclusion when the judge meticulously evaluated each web page. She assessed them on various fronts, including aesthetics, creativity, theme incorporation, functionality, content quality, and the overall impact they had on the viewers.
