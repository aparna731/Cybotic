Branch Inaugration

24/11/22

Cybotic, our esteemed student association, recently celebrated the grand inauguration of its new branch, marking a significant milestone in our journey. This momentous occasion symbolized our relentless commitment to fostering innovation and learning among tech enthusiasts. As we expanded our horizons, we were thrilled to present an engaging event for our members and beyond.
The day commenced with the Branch Inauguration, an event that marked a new chapter in Cybotic's history. Our attendees had the privilege of exploring our cutting-edge facilities and connecting with the dedicated team behind our success, providing an excellent opportunity for networking among like-minded tech enthusiasts.

Following the inauguration, our focus shifted to the Technical Seminar on Blockchain for Cyber Security in Internet of Things (IoT). In an era where data security is paramount, we delved into the fascinating world of blockchain technology and its pivotal role in safeguarding IoT. Our expert speakers shared invaluable insights into the potential of blockchain to revolutionize cybersecurity.
Throughout the seminar, we explored various aspects of blockchain, including its fundamental principles, real-world applications in cybersecurity, and compelling use cases in IoT security. Attendees were given a glimpse into the future of blockchain technology and its ever-expanding implications for IoT security.

Our distinguished speakers, renowned in their fields, provided attendees with valuable knowledge and perspectives. The event, open to students, tech enthusiasts, and professionals alike, aimed to bridge the knowledge gap and provide a platform for all to explore emerging technologies.
Registration for the event was mandatory due to limited seating, but it was completely free. Attendees secured their spots through our registration portal, ensuring their participation in this enlightening day of learning, networking, and celebration.
We encouraged all individuals interested in emerging technologies, cybersecurity, and blockchain to join us on this exciting journey. Cybotic was committed to promoting innovation and knowledge sharing, and we looked forward to welcoming everyone to our inaugural event.
